Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't meet all the requirements?

+

Don't worry! Our admissions team reviews each application individually. If you're missing some requirements, we may offer alternative pathways like additional pre-work or bridging courses to help you succeed.

Can I apply with a different educational background?

+

Absolutely! We welcome students from all educational backgrounds. Our programme is designed to accommodate learners from humanities, business, science, and other fields. Your diverse perspective can be a valuable asset.

Is there an age limit for the programme?

+

There's no age limit! We've had successful students ranging from recent graduates to career changers in their 50s. What matters most is your commitment and enthusiasm to learn.

Do I need programming experience to start?

+

No prior programming experience is required. Our pre-work phase and early modules are designed to bring everyone up to speed, regardless of their technical background.

What's the difference between full-time and part-time?

+

Full-time is 12 weeks, Monday-Friday, 9 AM-5 PM for intensive learning. Part-time is 24 weeks with evening classes (2 nights + 1 Saturday) to accommodate working professionals.

Can I switch between full-time and part-time?

+

Switches are possible before the programme starts, subject to availability. Once the course begins, changes are limited due to different pacing and scheduling requirements.

What if I miss classes due to personal commitments?

+

All sessions are recorded and available for later viewing. We also offer catch-up sessions and one-on-one support to ensure you don't fall behind in your learning.

Are classes held in local time zones?

+

Yes, we run multiple cohorts to accommodate different time zones. All times mentioned are in New Zealand Standard Time (NZST), but international students are welcome.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept credit cards, bank transfers, and payment plans. For the deposit option, you can use any major credit card or arrange a bank transfer.

Can I get a refund if I need to withdraw?

+

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee from the programme start date. After this period, refunds are calculated on a pro-rata basis for unused portions of the course.

Are there scholarships or financial aid available?

+

We offer need-based scholarships and early bird discounts. Additionally, some students may be eligible for government funding or employer sponsorship programs.

How long is the early bird discount valid?

+

Early bird pricing (20% off) is available for enrollments made at least 6 weeks before the cohort start date. This discount applies to both payment options.
