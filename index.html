Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our programmes.

What programmes do you offer?

+

We offer three industry-leading programmes: Data Science & AI, Cyber Security, and Software Engineering. Each programme is available in both full-time (12 weeks) and part-time (24 weeks) formats.

Do I need prior experience in tech?

+

No prior tech experience is required. Our programmes are designed for career changers and include comprehensive pre-work to bring everyone up to speed, regardless of their background.

What is the job placement success rate?

+

We have a 93% job outcome success rate within 180 days of graduation for active job seekers. Over 1000+ employers have hired our certified professionals.

Are the programmes taught online?

+

Yes, all programmes are available remotely with an interactive format featuring virtual break-out rooms, shared screens, digital whiteboards, and full instructor support.

What certifications will I receive?

+

You'll receive both a University of Canterbury Tuihono UC Online Certificate of Completion and an Institute of Data Industry Certification, signaling to employers that you're ready for the industry.

Is job placement support included?

+

Yes, all programmes include comprehensive job outcomes support with dedicated career coaches, industry networking events, and targeted employer engagement to help you land your ideal role.
